About Us

Who We Are

At Our Community in Unity, we're a nonprofit collective dedicated to community empowerment through technology, automation, and skill-based training. Founded in 2024 and based in Bonteheuwel, Cape Town, our mission is to bridge gaps in opportunity and income using hands-on education and innovative solutions.

Our Story

Born from a vision to tackle rising unemployment and crime, we began by offering mosaic apprenticeships and livestream support. Today, we operate a full digital hub, providing AI-driven business tools and peer networks that have helped over 500 locals start micro-enterprises.

Our Vision

A thriving community where every member has the tools, knowledge, and support to build sustainable livelihoods—and where local collaboration fuels lasting change.

Empowering Local Businesses Through Strategic Partnerships

At Our Community in Unity, we believe in the transformative power of collaboration. We partner with small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) to provide end-to-end sales and marketing solutions that drive growth and resilience.

We specialize in crafting and managing data-driven email marketing campaigns that resonate with your audience. Utilizing platforms like BossMailer, Mailchimp, and others, we ensure your message reaches the right people at the right time, fostering engagement and conversion.

Understanding that a business is only as strong as its team, we provide and equip SMEs with trained staff across various fields. From marketing strategists to customer service representatives, our personnel are prepared to meet your business needs.
