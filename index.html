About Us

Our Purpose

At Our Community in Unity, we are driven by a profound commitment to fostering growth, sustainability, and positive transformation within our community. Our journey began with a clear purpose: to build a united and resilient community through empowerment, education, and shared resources.

Our work is deeply rooted in our motto, "Each One Teach One," a principle that guides every initiative and interaction. We believe that by empowering individuals with knowledge and support, they in turn can uplift others, creating a powerful ripple effect of positive change.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To build a united and resilient community by empowering individuals through education, support, and shared resources, creating opportunities for growth and sustainable development.

Vision: To see a community where every individual is empowered, opportunities abound, and sustainable solutions drive social and cultural transformation.

Our Founder's Journey

Cameron De Vries, Founder and Director, embodies the spirit of 'Each One Teach One.' Despite leaving formal schooling in Grade 9, his insatiable drive for knowledge led to a remarkable journey of self-education and mastery in fields like sound engineering, construction, and business development.

His journey is a living example that skills, determination, and the willingness to learn and share knowledge can create profound opportunities. This unique blend of diverse experience and a deep understanding of community needs drives our organization forward.

Cameron is supported by Bertha De Vries (Co-Founder) and Antonio Clifford Marais (Director), who are vital for our organizational compliance and strategic oversight.

What We Do: Our Programs & Impact

Our comprehensive programs are designed to uplift and empower every individual in our community. We provide essential support, innovative training, and sustainable solutions, all guided by the principle of Each One Teach One.

Education & Training

Our workshops and training initiatives embody 'Each One Teach One,' fostering environments where skills are shared and knowledge is built collectively.

  • Digital Literacy & Innovation: AI Prompting, Email Marketing, Online Safety.
  • Job Readiness & Vocational Skills: CV Writing, Interview Prep, Professional Communication.
  • Financial Literacy: Basic Budgeting & Saving.

Social Support & Empowerment

Through mentoring and resource sharing, we facilitate connections where 'Each One Teaches One,' providing crucial support and guidance for upliftment.

  • Mentorship programs for youth and unemployed individuals.
  • Access to essential community resources.
  • Counseling and support services.

Sustainable Development

Our initiatives promote 'Each One Teach One' by sharing best practices for environmental conservation and social housing, building a sustainable future together.

  • Environmental conservation projects.
  • Support for sustainable housing solutions.
  • Community development projects.
